# Brain External Research Dispatch Contract

Use when Brain needs external facts before PRD, routing, stage, or scope decisions can be made.

Packet title:

Brain Dispatch: External Research

Required fields:
- Research Goal
- Question
- Why it matters
- Preferred sources
- Out of scope
- Expected output

Rules:
- Ask for facts, examples, constraints, standards, or source-backed findings.
- Do not ask the research agent to make product decisions owned by Brain.
- Keep the request narrow.
- Include source expectations when source quality matters.
